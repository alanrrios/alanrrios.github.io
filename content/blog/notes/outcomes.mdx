## Established Sytems

There's a system that I had to learn early on in my *career*, if one could call it that, that revolved around optimizing for.. caloric intake. It's that I was on a diet; it's that I could not afford to feed to myself. I went from being a competitive swimmer on an excessive caloric diet to maintain the training regime that demanded to scavenging for food and discounts. 

That system was [Calories by the Dollar](/notes/poverty): how many calories I was able to get for each dollar I spent. I experimented with different discounts and coupons to divide the total calories by the total cost of the meal.
